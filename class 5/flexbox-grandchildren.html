💡
The parent has display: flex, so its direct children become flex items and respond to flex properties. But the grandchildren inside those children are in normal flow — they stack vertically as block elements, completely ignoring the parent's flex context. To make grandchildren flex too, you'd need to add display: flex to their own parent (a child).
